Mac & Cheese

Description

Amazing and simple dish for two. Best while hot.

Ingredients

  • 250g of pasta
  • 100g of cheese
  • Olive oil or butter

Steps

  1. Add some salt and water to a boiling pan.
  2. Boil the pasta until it's al'dente.
  3. While it's boiling, get the cheese ready and make it small for the pasta.
  4. Take out the pasta, add cheese, olive oil or butter. Mix everything together.
  5. Serve with love! Eat with pleasure.
